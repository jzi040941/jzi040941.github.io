Waggle chicken - barbecue chicken:Fillow




I wanted to eat Texas fork BBQ but it was too late at night. So
I considered a similar one that is a chicken barbecue.

waggle chicken locates nearby my house. So I had no time to lose.

First I arrived at that chicken house. It seems like a pub. the Song I hearing so much what I like is coming out.

and look at this beer machine. don’t you want to go with a friend and have some drink? haha

I ordered chicken bbq it’s 18bucks. but you can get a 2bucks discount if you take it away.

It looks sososososo deliciouss and really delicious.
actually, I think I just eat half of that but ate it all.
it so chewy sweety and smokey. it’s the best chicken in my entire life

one more good thing they give free Pepsi. so great to people who Pepsi holic as I do
