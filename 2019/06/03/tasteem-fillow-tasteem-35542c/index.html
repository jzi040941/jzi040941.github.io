sabor peruano :Fillow



Hi, there

as I wanted to find Mudshake vodka. (sweet chocolate flavor amazing ever I feel entirely in my life😄)
I search for buying the vodka finally well-known vodka shop is located in the pyeongteak international market street.
but the shop is shut down.
freaking frustrated 😟 and hungry.
want to eat some special cuisine as I walk so much for getting this international market and felt I have to eat foreign food.
that’s why I go to this Peru restaurant in Korea.

the menu.
actually can’t understand what the food is even is written in Korean and English.
all description seems same 🤣🤣

I ordered taculomo and glass of drink

first, they give chip and dipping sauce.
fabulous experience in my life I’ve never dipped the chip into this kind of sauce.
want to eat again in my home.

mix rice with beef and beens blablablablablabla dont know what is it hahahaha.
it’s delicious and the beef is so nice but little expensive
15 boxes.
contrast to other Korean restaurant food price is 7to10 boxes.

and the chicha morada. its match with main dishes.
I was so curious what is made of.
then I asked owner she kindly told me is made of purple corn and top bubbles are apple.

recommand to visit if you are in international street and want to eat foreign cuisine.

but not just for dinner too expensive 😑

Thanks to stopping for my
